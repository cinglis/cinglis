# About me.

I'm a Project Manager at EPCC, the UK's first National Supercomputing centre, based at the University of Edinburgh.
- Before Brexit, I worked on a variety of EC-funded projects, primarily as co-ordinator of EPCC's Transnational Access visitor programmes.
- I now have a variety of roles in the ARCHER2 national HPC service, being part of both the User Administration team and the eCSE team, as well as contributing to outreach, impact and benefits realisation activities. I am also involved in organising the annual EPCC HPC Summer School.
- I am also a member of the Software Sustainability Institute (SSI), where I am a Deputy Community Manager in the Fellowship Programme team.
- I have created this page as part of the process of learning how to use GitHub.
